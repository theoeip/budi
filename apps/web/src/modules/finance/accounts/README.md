# Accounts

> Bank and cash account management.

## Purpose

Manages financial accounts for the school:

- Bank accounts (checking, savings)
- Cash accounts
- Digital wallets (future)
- Account balance tracking

## Components (Future)

- `AccountList` — Account cards/table
- `AccountForm` — Add/edit account form
- `AccountBalance` — Balance display with history
